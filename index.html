Green and fresh foods, such as salads, are vibrant, nutrient-rich, and bursting with natural flavors. They are composed of crisp vegetables, leafy greens, and fresh fruits, offering a wholesome blend of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. These foods not only provide essential nutrients but also promote a sense of lightness and freshness, promoting well-being and rejuvenation.
